What is financial literacy?

Financial literacy is the ability to understand and use various financial skills, including personal financial management, budgeting and investing. Every entrepreneur need to a good working knowledge of finances and financial management which includes how to pay his/her bills, how to borrow and save money responsibility, how and why to invest, and plan and report financial performance.

Why an entrepreneur has to be financially literate?

In order to start a business and stay in business, you’ll need to experience financial growth at some points like; with the money coming in and going out, with due dates and finance charges and fees attached to invoices and bills and with the overall responsibility of making the right decisions about major purchases and investments consistently.

If you learn that variety of important financial skills and concepts you will be able to deal with all that and be less vulnerable to financial fraud because it will prevent devastating financial mistakes, prepares you for emergencies, help you reach their goals and invokes your confidence and you will also be able to find what areas are affecting your revenue.

As you grow your business, you may hire someone with a better understanding of finances to handle that aspect of the business. But you still need a good working knowledge of all things about your money.
